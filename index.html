MOBILE VEHICLE VALETING

All aspects of vehicle valeting undertaken.

Competitive prices and services to meet all valeting requirements.

Fully self-sufficient van with pure water and power.

Call: 07531 414 789

We only use purified water. Tap water is processed through a resin filtration system and the total dissolvable solids that are found in tap water are greatly reduced. Hence this purified water leaves no residue on the car.

We comply with a 3 bucket system. Single use bucket for wash with shampoo/conditioner. Using non contaminated cloths, mitt and tools. Single use bucket for rinse and again a single bucket for wheel cleaning.

Our van can be utilised as a complete self sufficient vehicle. We have a water tank for the purified water that we source locally. We have a petrol fuelled generator that can power a plumbed in jet wash system with wet and dry vacuuming capabilities.

We use Auto Glym and Autosmart products from local distributors. Trained by Autosmart on procedures and usage of their products.
