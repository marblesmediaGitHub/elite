Mini Valet

The Mini Valet begins with a thorough rinse, followed by snow foam 'active foam' process and further rinse. The car is then hand washed, rinsed and microfiber towel dried. The external glass, door sills and door shuts are cleaned. On the inside, the Mini Valet includes hoovering of carpets and floor mats, dusting of dashboard and controls.

Maintenance Valet

The Maintenance Valet begins with a thorough rinse, followed by snow foam 'active foam' process and further rinse. The vehicle is then hand washed, rinsed and microfiber towel dried. Door sills and door shuts are cleaned. All glass is cleaned inside and out. On the inside, all seats, carpet and floor mats are vacuumed. The dashboard is cleaned and dressed, interior deodorised.

Gold Valet

The Gold Valet begins with a thorough rinse, followed by snow foam 'active foam' process and further rinse. The car is then hand washed, rinsed and microfiber towel dried. The engine bay is cleaned and dressed. This is followed by decontamination stage of road tar and impurity’s from paintwork. Door sills and door shuts are cleaned. All glass is cleaned inside and out. All seats, carpets and mats are vacuumed and dressed. The dashboard is cleaned and dressed, interior deodorised. Exterior body wax polish including all exterior plastics dressed.

Micro Car - £70

Medium Car - £75

4x4 SUV - £85
